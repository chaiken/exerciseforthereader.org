Mission Peak Birds

Very Common (constantly seen):

  • Oregon Junco
  • Turkey Vulture
  • Scrub Jay
  • Mourning Dove
  • Cliff Swallow

Common (almost every hike):

  • Western Bluebird (grassy areas)
  • Horned Lark (near summit)
  • Meadowlark (grassy areas)
  • American Kestrel (grassy areas)
  • Yellow-billed Magpie (Sunol parking lot)
  • Brown Towhee (under trees)
  • House Finch (under trees)
  • Bushtit (trees)
  • Red-Tailed Hawk (grassy areas)

Occasional (now and then or just seasonal):

  • Hermit Thrush (wooded areas)
  • California Quail (under trees)
  • Loggerhead Shrike (fences)
  • House Wren (wooded areas)
  • Anna's Hummingbird
  • Red-Shafted Flicker (trees)
  • Mallards (seasonal ponds)
  • Prairie Falcons (grassy areas)
  • Woodpeckers (Hairy? Downy?)
  • mystery sparrows (Song?) in grassy areas
  • Yellow-Rumped Warbler (wooded areas)
  • Red-Shouldered Hawk (eucalyptus at Stanford Ave. trailhead)
  • Wild Turkey (Monument Peak)

More to add? Corrections? Send email to alison@exerciseforthereader.org (Alison Chaiken) and let me know.
