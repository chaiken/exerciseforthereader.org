Papers about Intercalation Compounds

Some of these papers were written quite a few years ago, so I haven't taken the time to format them as nicely as the more recent papers. None of these papers have on-line figures and parts of the thesis are still a bit jumbled.

  • Anisotropic Superconductivity in C4KHg Phys. Rev. B41, 71 (1990). (3K)
  • The Superconducting Properties Of Ternary Graphite Intercalation Compounds, Dept. of Physics, MIT (1988) (9K) and the gzipped LaTex source (900K).
  • Enhanced Superconductivity in Hydrogenated Potassium--Mercury Graphite Intercalation Compounds Phys. Rev. B32, 533 (1985). (2K)

Fits to HC2(T) data on superconductors using the Werthamer-Helfand-Hohenberg (WHH) formula were performed with a C program that was written by J.E. Tkaczyk and J.A.X. Alexander and has been modified by me. Orbital, Pauli and magnetic impurity pair-breakers are included and thin-film geometries are allowed. The tarred and zipped sources are available by anonymous ftp. (19K)
