Maine Wilderness Canoe Basin Alumni Page

Maine Wilderness Canoe Basin alumni of the 1970's, we know you're out there. I was a camper and CIT from about 1976-1979 and participated in canoeing, kayaking and bicycling trips on the St. Croix, Allagash, Mattawamkeag and St. John Rivers, plus outings in the chain-of-lakes, Novia Scotia, New Brunswick, Acadia National Park and Baxter State Park. Remarkably MWCB no longer seems to exist despite the fact that the dreaded Camp Chewonki still is going strong.

In the last few years, I've been in touch with MWCB alumni Sara Low, Nan Burns, Susan McCabe and Karen Butts. Bruce and Cheryl B., Tom and Carol C., Ethan M., Brian B., Samantha, Doug H., E.J., Karen S., Eva M., are you out there in the ether? Anyone have reminiscences or digital photos they want to share?
