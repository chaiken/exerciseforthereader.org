Stuff I wholeheartedly endorse:

  • Beautiful Foods, the Bay Area's first catering service that is accessible over IPv6
  • Grameen Bank, originators of the world microlending movement that turns the poor into enterpreneurs; spends 83.7% of its income on its mission
  • In Peru, the Institute for Liberty and Democracy, a Third World advocate for reform of legal conditions that disfavor the poor
  • La Unica Wilderness Resort, in lovely Baja California, site of two of our best-ever vacations
  • The Physics Chanteuse, Lynda Williams. Whatever this is, the world needs more of it.
  • the Pretenders Archive
  • Epicurious, a searchable recipe database; also try the rec.food.recipes archive
  • Check out Liz Miller's in-line skating tips and clinics
  • Real Beer Page
  • Red Cross, spends 91% of income on charitable work
  • Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors without Borders), spends 86% of income on its mission
  • KALW 91.7, best Bay Area NPR station, especially the eclectic Tangents music program and the Friends of KALW
  • Help the Bay Area Ridge Trail Council build a ring of trails around the Bay
  • East Bay Regional Park District and the custodial Regional Parks Association (not the Regional Parks *Foundation*)
  • California State Parks info and reservations
  • Northern California National Forests link. Logout, go outside! (Unfortunately this website is often down.)
  • Listen to KPIG's live, low-ad content streaming mp3 webcast, featuring roots and blues music. Or try KFJC's ad-free streaming mp3 webcast.
  • Bay Area Concert Guide, an excellent service from Graham Spencer
  • Bay Nature magazine, a beautifully illustrated, thoughtfully written quarterly
  • California Coastal Conservancy, the folks who made the beaches accessible to you; publishers of the excellent California Coast and Ocean magazine
  • The Marin Agricultural Land Trust is proving that traditional adversaries can unite to pursue a common goal, the preservation of agricultural land. Why don't we have an Alameda County Agricultural Land Trust?
